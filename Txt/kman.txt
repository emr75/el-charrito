<p><strong>Kitchen Manager</strong></p>
<span> Like general managers, kitchen managers are responsible for hiring and firing employees, buying supplies and ingredients, and ensuring quality. However, a general manager controls both kitchen employees and front-of-house employees, whereas a kitchen manager only manages back-of-house operations.</span>  
<p><em>Job Requirements:</em></p>
<ol>
<li>Excellent people skills.</li>
<li>Work under pressure.</li>
<li>May require 3-4 years experience.</li>
</ol>
