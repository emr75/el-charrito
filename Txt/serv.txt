<p><strong>Server</strong></p>
<span> A good server can make or break the customer experience. Responsible for taking orders in a friendly manner, reporting orders to the kitchen, and calculating the bill, servers play an essential role in any casual or fine dining restaurant.</span>
<p><em>Job Requirements:</em></p>
<ol>
<li>Excellent memory.</li>
<li>Excellent people skills.</li>
<li>Ability to work quickly and efficiently.</li>
</ol>
