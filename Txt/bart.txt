<p><strong>Bartender</strong></p>
<span> Restaurant bartenders may serve customers directly or give their creations to servers for delivery, but either way, they must have an excellent memory and work well under pressure. A formal <em>bartending education</em> may be required at some restaurants, but many will hire based on experience.</span>
<p><em>Job Requirements:</em></p>
<ol>
<li>Excellent memory.</li>
<li>Ability to work quickly and efficiently.</li>
<li>Excellent people skills.</li>
</ol>
