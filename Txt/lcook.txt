<p><strong>Line Cook</strong></p>
<span> Although the duties differ depending upon the establishment, line cooks can be found in most restaurants, excluding fast food. A line cook may be responsible for one or multiple areas of the kitchen, such as the grill or fryer, depending upon the size and scale of the restaurant.</span>  
<p><em>Job Requirements:</em></p>
<ol>
<li>Works well with others.</li>
<li>Ability to work quickly and efficiently.</li>
<li>Experience in restaurant is an asset.</li>
</ol>
